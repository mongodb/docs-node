The **Monitoring** section contains detailed information to enable you
to observe driver operations for telemetry use-cases.

* :doc:`Cluster Monitoring </fundamentals/monitoring/cluster-monitoring>`: configure the driver to
  monitor MongoDB server discovery and monitoring events

* :doc:`Command Monitoring </fundamentals/monitoring/command-monitoring>`: configure the driver to
  monitor all executed commands
